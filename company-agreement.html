Saiqi Company Platform Agreement. This Agreement governs registration, access, and use of the Saiqi mobile application, company dashboard, websites, software, communication systems, ride-management systems, and related services, collectively referred to as the Platform.
Section 01

Scope and Parties

This Agreement is entered into between Ahmad Mamoun Ibrahim Amous, located in Tulkarem, Palestine, as the owner and operator of the Saiqi Platform—referred to as Saiqi, the Platform, we, us, or our—and the transportation company, taxi office, fleet operator, licensed transportation provider, or other business registering for a Company account—referred to as the Company, you, or your.

The person completing registration on behalf of the Company is the Company Representative.

Section 02

Electronic Acceptance and Authority

By actively selecting the required acceptance checkbox and pressing Create Company Account, Register, Accept and Continue, or a similar registration button, the Company and Company Representative confirm that they have read, understood, and agreed to be legally bound by this Agreement.

The Company Representative confirms authority to register and legally bind the Company. Actively ticking the required checkbox is an affirmative act of consent. Electronic records may be used as evidence of acceptance. Version 1 becomes binding when accepted.

Section 03

Nature of the Platform

Saiqi is a technology and coordination platform. Saiqi is not the provider of the transportation service, not a taxi company, not the owner or operator of the Company's vehicles, and not the employer of the Company's drivers.

The Company provides transportation through its drivers and vehicles and remains responsible for that service.

Section 04

Company Eligibility and Compliance

The Company represents that it:

  • Is legally established or permitted to conduct its activities.
  • Holds required transportation and business permissions.
  • Provides accurate and current information and documents.
  • Will comply with applicable laws and regulatory requirements.
Section 05

Company Responsibilities

The Company is responsible for:

  • Its account and account security.
  • Its drivers, vehicles, licenses, insurance, and legal compliance.
  • Ride offers, accepted rides, fares, additional charges, and customer service.
  • The transportation service provided through its drivers and vehicles.
  • Informing every driver about location, availability, ride, income, and performance monitoring.
  • Ensuring drivers accept the separate Driver Agreement and Privacy Policy.
Section 06

Operational Data and Access

The Company agrees Saiqi may collect, process, display, and access operational data needed to run the Platform, including:

Company, driver, and vehicle informationDriver location and availabilityLive-ride information and ride historyRide counts and faresDriver-attributed Platform incomeCompany Platform-generated revenueRatings and complaintsPerformance and operational records
Section 07

Platform Commission

Commercial term

10% of the gross fare

The Company agrees to pay Saiqi a ten percent (10%) Platform commission on the gross fare of every completed ride generated through Saiqi.

The Company—not the assigned driver—is responsible for the commission on rides performed under the Company Account. Cash or outside payments do not remove this obligation.

Section 08

Verification and Documents

Saiqi may request company licenses, registration papers, owner or representative identity documents, taxi or transport permissions, vehicle records, insurance records, driver documents, tax records, banking information, and other verification documents.

False, altered, stolen, expired, or inconsistent documents may lead to suspension or termination.

Section 09

Suspension or Termination

Saiqi may suspend or terminate a Company Account for:

  • Breach of this Agreement or Platform policies.
  • Unpaid commission or commission avoidance.
  • Fraud, manipulation, or misuse of Platform data.
  • Missing, expired, false, or inconsistent documents.
  • Safety risk, serious complaints, or legal ineligibility.
  • Any material legal, financial, security, operational, or reputational risk.
Section 10

Changes to This Agreement

Saiqi may amend this Agreement. Where appropriate or legally required, the Company may be required to actively accept a new version through a checkbox or similar action.

Section 11

Governing Law and Disputes

This Agreement is governed by the laws applicable in the State of Palestine. Disputes should first be handled through good-faith written communication. Unresolved disputes may be submitted to competent Palestinian courts.

Section 12

Company's Express Confirmations

By accepting, the Company confirms that:

  • Its registration information is true and it is authorized to provide the registered services.
  • The Company Representative has authority to bind the Company.
  • It has read and understood this Agreement and accepts responsibility for its drivers and vehicles.
  • It understands Saiqi is a technology Platform, not the transportation provider.
  • It accepts the 10% commission on every completed ride generated through Saiqi.
  • It understands the Company—not the driver—owes commission for rides under its account.
  • It understands cash and outside payments do not remove the commission obligation.
  • It understands Saiqi may access the operational information described above.
  • It will notify drivers about monitoring and ensure they accept the Driver Agreement and Privacy Policy.
  • It accepts electronic recordkeeping, Saiqi policies, and applicable law.
Section 13

Required Registration Acceptance

The following acknowledgement is presented during company registration:

I confirm that I am authorized to act on behalf of the Company. I have read and agree to the Saiqi Company Platform Agreement, Version 1, the Privacy Policy, and the applicable fee terms. I understand that the Company must pay Saiqi a 10% commission on the gross fare of every completed ride generated through Saiqi. I also understand that Saiqi collects and may access Driver locations, availability, live-ride information, ride history, ride counts, fares, Driver-attributed Platform income, Company Platform-generated revenue, performance information, and related operational records as explained in this Agreement. By ticking this box and completing registration, I electronically accept these terms on behalf of the Company.
