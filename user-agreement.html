How to use this draft

  • This agreement is written for passengers/users, not drivers or taxi companies.

  • User acceptance should be recorded with agreement version, timestamp, user identifier, language, and a tamper-resistant audit record.

Section 02

Acceptance and electronic consent

By creating an account, selecting an acceptance checkbox, tapping "Agree and continue", requesting a ride, or otherwise using the Platform after this Agreement is presented to you, you confirm that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by this Agreement and the documents incorporated into it.

Your electronic acceptance has the same effect as a handwritten signature to the extent permitted by applicable law. If you do not agree, do not create an account, request a ride, or use the Platform.

If the person using the Platform lacks legal capacity to enter this Agreement, a parent, guardian, or other legally authorized person must accept on that person's behalf where the law permits. Saiqi may require evidence of authority.

Section 03

Definitions

  • "Driver" means a person authorized through the Platform to receive or perform ride requests, normally through a registered Taxi Company.
  • "Taxi Company" means a transport company or fleet account registered on the Platform that may receive requests, submit offers, assign drivers, and manage vehicles.
  • "Ride" means a passenger transport service requested or arranged using the Platform.
  • "Ride Offer" means an offer or proposed ride arrangement shown through the Platform, which may include a proposed fare, estimated arrival time, Driver, vehicle, or Taxi Company information.
  • "User Content" means information, text, ratings, comments, images, support messages, or other material submitted by a User.
  • "Applicable Law" means all mandatory laws, regulations, orders, and licensing requirements that apply to the relevant person, Ride, territory, or service.
Section 04

Eligibility and authority

You may use the Platform only if you are at least [MINIMUM PASSENGER AGE], have legal capacity to enter a binding agreement, and are not prohibited from using the Platform under Applicable Law.

You may request a Ride for another person only if you are authorized to do so, provide accurate pickup and contact information, ensure the passenger is informed of relevant Ride details, and accept responsibility for charges and conduct associated with the request. Special rules may apply to minors and persons requiring assistance.

The Platform is not designed for unaccompanied minors unless Saiqi expressly launches and documents such a service. Do not request an unaccompanied-minor Ride unless the Platform specifically permits it and all stated conditions are satisfied.

Section 05

Accounts, authentication, and security

You must provide accurate, current, and complete registration information and keep it updated. The Platform may support authentication by phone number, one-time password, email, password, Google, or another method made available in your territory.

  • Maintain control of your phone, email account, credentials, and verification codes.
  • Do not share one-time passwords or allow another person to use your account.
  • Notify Saiqi promptly if you suspect unauthorized use, account takeover, or loss of your device.
  • Use only one genuine account unless Saiqi expressly permits additional accounts.
  • Provide a valid phone number before requesting a Ride when the Platform requires it.

You are responsible for activity conducted through your account unless Applicable Law provides otherwise. Saiqi may request identity, phone, or device verification and may refuse or restrict access when verification cannot be completed.

Section 06

Nature of the Platform

Saiqi provides technology that helps Users request Rides, locate nearby transport providers, receive or review Ride Offers, communicate operational details, track Ride status, and maintain a Ride history.

Unless Saiqi expressly states otherwise in writing for a specific service, Saiqi is not the carrier and does not itself provide the physical transportation. Transportation is provided by the accepting Taxi Company and/or Driver. Those providers are responsible for operating the vehicle, holding required licenses and insurance, complying with traffic and transport laws, and performing the Ride safely and professionally.

Nothing in this Agreement removes any non-excludable responsibility Saiqi has under Applicable Law. The legal characterization of Saiqi, a Taxi Company, or a Driver may vary by jurisdiction and cannot be changed by contract where mandatory law provides otherwise.

Section 07

Ride requests, offers, and matching

When you submit a Ride request, the Platform may transmit it to one or more eligible Taxi Companies and/or Drivers, depending on location, availability, service mode, technical conditions, and operational rules.

A Ride Offer may display a proposed or estimated fare, estimated arrival time, Taxi Company, Driver, vehicle, rating, pickup point, destination, or other details. You may accept or reject an available offer as the Platform permits. A request is not guaranteed to result in an offer or completed Ride.

  • Check the pickup point, destination, selected provider, Driver, vehicle, plate information, and fare details before entering a vehicle.
  • Contact support if the arriving Driver or vehicle materially differs from the confirmed details.
  • Do not enter a vehicle if you reasonably believe it is unsafe or is not the vehicle assigned to the Ride.
  • Keep location and contact details sufficiently accurate for pickup and Ride completion.
Section 08

Estimates, routes, and arrival times

Maps, routes, distances, pickup estimates, arrival times, and trip durations are estimates. They may change because of traffic, road closures, weather, map data, GPS accuracy, User location, provider decisions, demand, or other conditions.

Saiqi does not guarantee that a particular route will be used, that a Driver will arrive at a stated time, or that a Ride will be completed within an estimate. The Driver remains responsible for lawful and safe driving and may change the route where reasonably necessary.

Section 09

Fares, currency, and payment

The current product records cash as the passenger payment method and displays fare information in ILS where configured. If additional currencies or payment methods are introduced, the Platform will identify them and any additional terms before use.

A fare shown before acceptance may be a provider offer or estimate rather than a guaranteed final amount. The final amount may change where disclosed and lawful, including because of changed destination, waiting, additional stops, route conditions, tolls, mandatory charges, or correction of an obvious error.

  • Review the offered or estimated fare before accepting a Ride.
  • Pay the Driver or Taxi Company using the method confirmed for the Ride.
  • Do not make duplicate payment where the Platform records a prior payment.
  • Request a receipt or raise a fare dispute through support as soon as reasonably possible.
  • Do not attempt charge avoidance, payment fraud, or manipulation of location, route, or fare data.
Section 10

Cancellations, no-shows, and unavailable Rides

You may cancel a request or Ride through the Platform when the cancellation function is available. The Platform may ask for a reason. A Driver or Taxi Company may also cancel or be unable to complete a Ride because of safety, access, vehicle, legal, operational, or other reasonable concerns.

Repeated false requests, abusive cancellations, intentional no-shows, or misuse of the cancellation process may lead to warnings, restrictions, recovery of disclosed costs, or account suspension where permitted by law.

Any cancellation fee, free-cancellation window, no-show fee, refund rule, and dispute period must be displayed or linked before it binds you. No cancellation fee is created solely by this draft.

Section 11

User conduct and prohibited activity

You must treat Drivers, Taxi Company personnel, other passengers, support staff, and the public with respect. You must comply with reasonable safety instructions and Applicable Law.

  • Do not submit false, duplicate, misleading, or fraudulent Ride requests.
  • Do not threaten, harass, assault, stalk, discriminate against, or abuse another person.
  • Do not damage, contaminate, or unlawfully interfere with a vehicle or other property.
  • Do not carry illegal items, hazardous materials, weapons prohibited by law, or items a provider reasonably refuses for safety.
  • Do not interfere with driving, encourage traffic violations, or request an unsafe pickup or drop-off.
  • Do not tamper with the Platform, reverse engineer it, introduce malicious code, scrape data, bypass security, or use unauthorized automation.
  • Do not misuse another person's personal data, live location, phone number, messages, or Ride information.
  • Do not use the Platform for unlawful transport, exploitation, trafficking, or any criminal activity.
Section 12

Safety and emergencies

Saiqi is not an emergency service.

If you face an immediate threat, medical emergency, crime, fire, collision, or other urgent danger, contact the appropriate local emergency authority first. Platform support cannot dispatch police, fire, or ambulance services and should not delay an emergency call.

  • Verify the Driver and vehicle details before boarding.
  • Wear a seat belt where available and required.
  • Choose a lawful and reasonably safe pickup and drop-off point.
  • Do not distract the Driver or pressure the Driver to violate traffic laws.
  • Report serious safety incidents to authorities and to Saiqi support when safe to do so.
  • Preserve relevant Ride details, photographs, medical records, or police references where appropriate.
Section 13

Driver and Taxi Company responsibilities

The Driver and/or Taxi Company providing a Ride is responsible for the transport service, including vehicle licensing, roadworthiness, insurance, driver authorization, traffic compliance, pickup, route decisions, conduct during the Ride, and any transport-specific legal duties.

Saiqi may establish onboarding, account, or operational controls, but those controls do not guarantee a provider's identity, licensing, insurance, behavior, availability, suitability, or future compliance. Users should exercise reasonable judgment and report material discrepancies.

Section 14

Ratings, feedback, and User Content

After a Ride, the Platform may allow you to rate a Driver and/or Taxi Company and submit comments. Providers may also rate Users. Ratings may be displayed only after a minimum number of reviews or may be aggregated to reduce identification risk.

  • Submit honest, relevant, first-hand, and respectful feedback.
  • Do not include threats, hate, harassment, unlawful accusations, confidential information, or unnecessary personal data.
  • Do not manipulate ratings, coordinate false reviews, or offer compensation for a rating.
  • Understand that Saiqi may moderate, restrict, remove, retain, or disclose content where reasonably necessary for safety, legal compliance, dispute handling, or Platform integrity.

You retain ownership of your User Content. You grant Saiqi a non-exclusive, worldwide, royalty-free license to host, store, reproduce, translate, format, moderate, and use that content only as reasonably necessary to operate, improve, protect, and promote the Platform, subject to the Privacy Policy and Applicable Law.

You may control permissions through device settings, but disabling required permissions may prevent Ride functions from working.

The Platform may also request notification, photo-library, camera, or other permissions for specific features. A permission request does not by itself authorize use beyond the purpose disclosed in the prompt and Privacy Policy.

Account deletion and retained records

Deletion does not cancel obligations or resolve unpaid amounts, pending disputes, or legal claims that arose before deletion. Where data cannot lawfully be deleted, access should be restricted and retention limited to the required purpose.

Schedule

Schedule 1 - Passenger Code of Conduct

Every passenger is expected to help keep Rides safe, respectful, and lawful.

  • Be ready at the confirmed pickup point and use a lawful, safe location.
  • Check the Driver, vehicle, and plate details before entering.
  • Treat the Driver, Taxi Company, support team, other passengers, and public with respect.
  • Use a seat belt and ensure children use legally required restraints.
  • Do not distract the Driver or request speeding, unsafe stopping, or another traffic violation.
  • Do not smoke, consume prohibited substances, carry unlawful goods, or damage the vehicle.
  • Tell the provider in advance about accessibility needs, unusually large luggage, animals, or other material requirements where the Platform supports this.
  • Pay the confirmed fare using the approved payment method and raise disputes honestly.
  • Protect personal information and never request or share verification codes.
  • Report serious misconduct, collisions, threats, or suspected fraud promptly and truthfully.

A serious or repeated breach may lead to Ride refusal, cleaning or damage claims where lawful and evidenced, account restriction, suspension, termination, reporting to authorities, or other proportionate action.
