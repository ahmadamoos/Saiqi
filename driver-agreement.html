This agreement governs your use of the Saiqi application and platform (the “Platform”) as a driver.

By ticking the approval box and selecting “Agree and continue”, you confirm that you have read, understood, and agree to comply with this agreement.
Section 01

Nature of the Saiqi Platform

Saiqi is a technology platform that connects passengers with transport companies and their registered drivers.

A passenger sends a ride request through the app. Transport companies may receive the request and submit offers. The passenger chooses the suitable offer, and after the offer is accepted, a driver belonging to the company may be assigned or suggested to complete the ride.

Saiqi does not own drivers' vehicles and is not considered the driver's employer unless a separate written agreement expressly states otherwise.

Section 02

Driver Status on the Platform

When registering, the driver is in one of the following statuses:

Company driver

Working under a transport company

A driver linked to an active, registered transport company account on Saiqi.

Independent

Approved independent driver

A driver whom Saiqi expressly allows to receive rides independently.

A. Driver working under a transport company

  1. The driver does not pay any commission, subscription, or direct usage fee to Saiqi for trips performed for the company.
  2. Saiqi deals financially with the transport company only. Any platform commission, fee, or subscription is owed by the company under a separate agreement.
  3. Payment of the driver's fare, salary, percentage, or commission is solely the responsibility of the transport company, according to its agreement with the driver.
  4. Saiqi is not responsible for any delay, dispute, or disagreement related to amounts paid by the company to the driver.
  5. The driver has no right to claim from Saiqi any salary, percentage, or financial entitlement for work with the company unless Saiqi expressly agrees in writing.
  6. If the company is suspended, ends its relationship with Saiqi, or removes the driver, the driver's account may be suspended until the driver joins another company or is approved as independent.

B. Independent driver

  1. Saiqi may charge an independent driver a commission, subscription fee, or operating fee.
  2. Commission or fees will be explained inside the app or in a separate financial policy before the independent account is activated.
  3. The independent driver agrees that a due commission or fee may be deducted from earnings or requested for payment in the manner defined by Saiqi.
  4. A driver does not become independent automatically after leaving a company; new approval and activation by Saiqi are required.
Section 03

No Fees for Company Drivers

Key financial acknowledgement

Company drivers are not charged directly by Saiqi.

As long as I am registered as a driver working under an active transport company in Saiqi, Saiqi will not ask me to pay any direct commission, subscription, or fee for trips I perform for that company.

I understand that any fees related to using the Platform are charged to the company, not to me personally.

I understand that any amounts, salary, or percentage I am entitled to for my work as a driver are the responsibility of the company I work with, not Saiqi.

Section 04

Driver and Vehicle Registration Conditions

The driver confirms that:

  1. The driver holds a valid driving license suitable for the vehicle type.
  2. The driver has all professional and legal permits required to transport passengers.
  3. The driver operates a licensed, insured, and technically roadworthy vehicle.
  4. The driver submitted accurate and up-to-date information and documents.
  5. The driver will update data immediately when any license, insurance, or document expires.
  6. The driver will not allow any other person to use the account.
  7. The driver will not use a vehicle different from the approved vehicle without approval or updating Platform records.
Section 05

Driver Obligations During Rides

The driver must:

  1. Respect passengers and deal with them professionally.
  2. Follow traffic and safety laws.
  3. Not drive under the influence of alcohol, drugs, or any substance that affects driving.
  4. Maintain vehicle cleanliness and safety.
  5. Not harass, threaten, abuse, or discriminate against any passenger.
  6. Not request additional amounts outside the amount shown or approved in the app.
  7. Not perform a ride outside the app with a passenger reached through Saiqi.
  8. Not share passenger data or contact passengers after a ride for personal or marketing purposes.
  9. Not manipulate location, distance, ride time, or fare amount.
  10. Immediately report accidents, security issues, or serious vehicle faults.
Section 06

Transport Company Responsibility

When the driver works under a transport company, the company is responsible for:

  1. Selecting its drivers and checking their eligibility.
  2. Paying drivers according to its agreement with them.
  3. Managing the professional or contractual relationship with drivers.
  4. Ensuring drivers and vehicles have the required licenses and insurance.
  5. Handling any financial or administrative dispute between the company and the driver.

Saiqi is not responsible for financial, labor, or administrative disputes between the company and the driver.

Section 07

Suspension or Deactivation

Saiqi may immediately suspend or deactivate a driver's account when there is a reason related to safety, law, or service quality, including:

  • Expired license, insurance, or permit.
  • Using incorrect or forged documents.
  • Sharing the account with another person.
  • Manipulating rides, prices, or location.
  • Assaulting, harassing, threatening, or abusing passengers.
  • Serious traffic violations.
  • Performing rides outside the Platform with Saiqi passengers.
  • Seriously violating company or Platform instructions.
  • A serious complaint or ongoing investigation.
Section 08

Data and Privacy

The driver agrees that Saiqi may collect and process data necessary to operate the service, such as:

  • Name, phone number, and identity data.
  • License, vehicle, and insurance data.
  • Location while using the app.
  • Ride history, offers, acceptances, and cancellations.
  • Ratings and complaints.
  • Payment or entitlement data when needed.

The driver must keep passenger data confidential and must not use it outside the scope of completing the ride.

Section 09

Electronic Acceptance Evidence

The driver acknowledges that electronic acceptance of this agreement is binding.

Saiqi keeps an electronic record that may include, when available:

  • Agreement version number.
  • Acceptance date and time.
  • Driver account number.
  • Phone number linked to the account.
  • IP address or device data.
  • Approval checkbox state.
  • Any later updates or acceptances.

The driver may review a copy of the agreement inside the app or request a copy from Saiqi support.

Section 10

Law and Disputes

This agreement is governed by the laws applicable in the State of Palestine.

Any dispute between the driver and Saiqi will first be attempted to be resolved through support or management. If an amicable solution is not possible, the competent courts in Tulkarem will have jurisdiction unless the law requires otherwise.
